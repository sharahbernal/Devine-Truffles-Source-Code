Classic Chocolate Truffles

Classic Chocolate Truffles are as decadent as they are simple to make. Yet they’re blissfully elegant and so delicious. They’re a luxurious treat that makes the perfect gift, or to serve as an after-dinner dessert.

Almond Truffles

Almond truffles have a double dose of almond flavor. The ganache is flavored with almond paste, and the finished truffles are rolled in a coating of crushed, toasted almonds. They’re creamy, melt-in-your-mouth and will satisfy all chocolate lovers’ cravings.

Oreo Strawberry Truffle

Oreo Strawberry Truffle are a fun twist on a classic favorite. These fun bite-sized treats are made with Golden Oreo cookies and freeze dried strawberries mixed with cream cheese and dipped in a delicious white chocolate coating.

Macha Truffle

Matcha truffles made from Japanese green tea powder are the perfect little chocolate treat. Intense flavors from the raspberry and outer coating of matcha go perfectly with the smooth and rich matcha green tea white chocolate.

Cookies & Cream Truffles

Cookies and Cream Truffles is made with cream cheese and chocolate are a perfect combo for melt-in-your-mouth truffles. Adding a little shortening to the chocolate as it melts makes the chocolate more easier to do the coating.

Oreo Macha Truffle

Oreo Matcha truffles made from Japanese green tea powder are the perfect little chocolate treat. Intense flavors from the oreo and outer coating of matcha go perfectly with the smooth and rich matcha green tea with oreo bits chocolate.
